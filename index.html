==FAQ==

What's Ethereal Division?

Ethereal Division is an electronic music producer that started in Febuary 2024, most known for their albums I Don't Even Know Anymore and Noise. Their first song, Negative Zero, dates back to May 8, 2024.

What should I listen to?

You should listen to Negative & Positive Zero to get introduced to the band. They are the oldest songs but they are good ones.

How do they make music?

They use the online music maker Beepbox. This website is basic but it did produce all of Ethereal Division's great songs.

Where can I listen to Ethereal Division?

Originally, Ethereal Division's music was only available on SoundCloud, but now you can listen to it on any of your favorite streaming services. Examples: Amazon Music, Spotify, Apple Music.

When is the new Ethereal Division album coming out?

Check the updates section for the most relevant information. Right now, the new release date is unknown but we do know an E.P. is coming up, possibly named Wasteland.
